About Us

CapitalSage is an integrated digital financial service group, providing people-driven empowerment and inclusive solutions for individuals and businesses for sustainable value and wealth creation in Africa.
We retail world-class technologies to African businesses and individuals so that they can prosper and realise their full potential. Our agenda is anchored on three significant pillars - financial inclusion, entrepreneurial development and wealth creation - steered by the force of innovative technology.

Residing in the most populous country on the most diverse continent, we quickly realised that conventional financial institutions are handicapped in creating sustainable value for the peculiar African society. This inspired our adoption of an innovative agile business model focused on people’s deep need and how we can create or retail seamless financial solutions to drive transformation, better experience and the value they seek. Our impressive portfolio of services include Agency banking, Digital savings and investments, value-added services (VAS), e-payment system, multi-level networking, point of Sales devices(MPOS/POS) and allied services. These don’t fully describe the depth of who we are,as we possess the deep intuition and willpower to go wherever the aspiration of our customers takes them and help them thrive.

We understand the task, that is why we are gulded intensely by strong values and constitute a versatile team of tech-heads, risk managers, engineers, financial analysts and unmatched relationship/support experts to help turn your dreams into amazing realities.

Our Journey

  • 2014 - Divine Mercy

    Started as an informal business under the name Divine Mercy. The mandate was empowering market women with loans.

  • 2018 - CreditAssist Investment Limited

    Consolidated our offerings after 4 years of successful operations and business boom. On July 2nd, 2018 the business officially registered as CreditAssist Investment Limited (RC:1507157) with footprints across the country.

  • 2020 - CapitalSage Group

    Business offerings expanded and strategically evolved from a micro-finance institution into a full-pool financial technology group servicing individuals, MSMEs, corporate organisations and government institutions. By September 8, 2020, we rebranded as CapitalSage with fully integrated digital services.

Our operations are duly licenced by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN)
